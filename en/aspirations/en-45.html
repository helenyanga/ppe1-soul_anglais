58 Soul Quotes And Sayings To Nurture Your Inner Spirit (2025)

BY Isabela Sedano , BEng
PUBLISHED: 01·18·24
UPDATED: 01·20·25
× ×
×

The concept of the soul is a deep-seated part of our collective human consciousness, reflected in various aspects of art, philosophy, and life. With multiple perspectives, it is not only complex but also illuminating. Explore our collection of soul quotes to tap into the wisdom cultivated over vast periods and diverse cultures.

Best Soul Quotes

The soul has been continuously explored in philosophy and religion for ages. In the modern context, it's broadly accepted as a spiritual entity, unique to each individual, that represents our character, emotion, and consciousness. 

Listening to our soul equates to discovering and following our identity despite the external noise. Here are some of our favorite quotes about soul that explore insights into why we should consider our inner souls.





Quotes On How To Make A Happy Soul

To have a cheerful soul, we must attend to our well-being. This can be done through genuine gratitude, a deep appreciation of nature, or a quick meditation session. Clearly, sustaining our happiness goes beyond physical wellness. Most of the time, it involves focusing on our inner selves.

The advice from Anne Frank to Mahatma Gandhi below keeps us in check to take care of our souls as we navigate our daily lives. Our next selection features quotes about nurturing a happy soul. For more tips, check our articles on connecting with nature and our list of mindfulness books.

Motivational Quotes To Light Up Our Souls

A passionate pursuit of dreams can set our souls ablaze. This fiery motivation fuels our dreams, propelling us towards unprecedented success. However, motivation is not forever, so we must practice discipline and have a productive system to keep our progress rolling.

Fire up your soul with this next batch of inspiring quotes.

Quotes About The Interconnectedness Of Love And Soul

Love is the everlasting string that binds our souls to our family, friends, and significant others. When we are more connected to a deeper level, understanding, patience, and all other ingredients of a successful relationship also expand.

However, we should first and foremost have a soul connection with ourselves. Practicing self-love attracts all the positivity in the world, from achieving dreams to long-lasting relationships. Here are our picks of soul quotes related to love.

Mysteries Of Our Souls Mirroring Our True Self

The choices we make and the philosophy we uphold loudly declare the condition of our souls. When we feed it with positivity, it exudes warmth. The opposite happens when it receives pessimistic thoughts.

The following quotes about the mysteries of the soul remind us to check our mirrors. Be a source of beautiful energy, and let the world take comfort and inspiration from your bright soul. 

A broken soul is not the absence of beauty, but a cracked and torn soul reeks of the sweet incense it contains.

~ C. JoyBell C.

Profound Sayings About The Soul From Great Minds

The concept of the soul has ancient roots tracing back to various perspectives held by philosophers, theologians, and scientists. Philosophers may view it as a manifestation of consciousness, while theologians see it as an eternal spiritual entity. 

Scientists, particularly Dr. Duncan Macdougall, attempted a more tangible method. His 1907 experiment involved weighing dying patients to ascertain the soul's weight. Remarkably, he documented a sudden weight loss of roughly 21 grams at death. However, his method has garnered skepticism.

These views show how our exploration of the meaning of the soul will continue eternally. Next up we feature some more philosophical quotes about the soul from renowned thinkers.

Never be in a hurry; do everything quietly and in a calm spirit. Do not lose your inner peace for anything whatsoever, even if your whole world seems upset.

~ Saint Francis de Sales

Noteworthy Soul Excerpts In Literature

Literature has long been an avenue for exploring the intricacies of the soul. Poe's melancholy ode to a lost love in "The Raven '' speaks of a despairing soul. Meanwhile, Dostoevsky in "The Brothers Karamazov '' defends the idea of the enduring human spirit.

In this section on the arts and literature, we feature soul quotes from various authors from their works, both historical and contemporary.

We have the same kind of stardust in our souls.

~ butterflies rising

You are a miracle of Divine Light, created by Divine Love and infused with Divine Wisdom to be a Soul having a human experience, right here and right now on planet earth.

~ Genevieve Gerard

The beautiful souls of the world have an art of saintly alchemy, by which bitterness is converted into kindness, the gall of human experience into gentleness, ingratitude into benefits, insults into pardon.

~ Henri Frederic Amiel

We ourselves feel that what we are doing is just a drop in the ocean. But the ocean would be less because of that missing drop.

~ Mother Teresa

Insights On The Importance Of Soul In Art

Art, in its most sincere form, expresses the artist's soul. Michael Jackson believed in the healing power of music that genuinely reaches into the soul. At the same time, Van Gogh dedicated his entire being, even his sanity, to his art. What is your favorite music or painting? Did it evoke something within your soul? This section of quotes is all about the soul in art to inspire the joys of life and compassion for other souls.

I was always an unusual girl. My mother told me I had a chameleon soul, no moral compass pointing due north, no fixed personality; just an inner indecisiveness that was as wide and as wavering as the ocean.

~ Lana Del Rey

Spirituality is seeded, germinates, sprouts and blossoms in the mundane. It is to be found and nurtured in the smallest of daily activities.

~ Thomas Moore (Care For the Soul)
