   Downtown Bristol has a new option for soul food and to-go meals with
   the opening of Legit's Kitchen and Catering.

   The restaurant’s menu includes creamy Alfredo, fresh salads and soul
   food plates with classic sides such as cornbread, collard greens, yams,
   and mac and cheese.

   Owner Christian Rippy said he has always loved to cook and is excited
   to share that passion with the community. “I offer food for your soul
   just because I cook it with soul, I cook it with love," he said. "I
   feel like when you eat my food, I feel like you are just happy. It's
   fulfilling.”

   Legit's Kitchen and Catering is focused on convenient, to-go style
   meals and is also available for catering.
   READ THE COMMENTS (1)

   The restaurant is located at 174 Moore St. and is open Tuesday through
   Saturday from 1 to 8.
