Lost Luggage, Redeemed Souls

   By Elder Gary E. Stevenson
--
   Youth outside a temple

   These precious souls are beings of potential beyond measure whose
   destiny it is to walk through eternity in halls of celestial glory.

--
   We each possess a tag that marks us as a child of a loving Heavenly
   Father. Printed upon that tag is a spiritual promise and sacred
   proclamation that testifies, “This precious soul has great worth to one
   day be redeemed by its holder,”^ never to be forgotten, neglected, or
   lost.
