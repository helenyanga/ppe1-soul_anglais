Physical Expressions of tHe Soul

   Human beings show their soul through physical expressions that can be
   seen and understood. It is new information for humanity that the soul
   can express itself in many different ways. These physical expressions
   are an opportunity to understand the soul, as it can be viewed in a
   person’s physicality.

   For example, a person’s movement shows their soul from birth. Every
   person is born with a Soultype, and that Soultype determines the area
   of the body from which all movement naturally originates throughout the
   course of a person’s entire life. Although the soul does not “live” in
   any single place in the body, the Soultype location serves as the point
   of origin for a person’s movement patterns.

   However, movement is only one way the soul becomes visible. The soul
   can also be expressed in other ways, such as through one’s eyes, muscle
   tone, facial expressions, speech, and breathing patterns. All of these
   can reveal aspects of the soul if you know what you are looking for.

   Although these ideas may seem conceptually straightforward, it requires
   training to accurately recognize the soul in these expressions. For
   example, an aspect of the Facial Expression of Strength for Soultype 3
   includes a smile. But the Soultype 3 smile has distinct qualities, and
   a smile from someone of another Soultype is not an expression of their
   soul. Every Soultype has something completely unique to bring to the
   world, and every Soultype expresses themselves entirely differently
   than the other eight.

   We know that the use of the word soul can often carry connotations.
   However, it is the word that most accurately expresses the magnitude of
   one’s spirituality and physicality as it is connected to all of
   existence. Each and every soul is beautiful and has something divinely
   unique to bring to humanity.
