New soul food restaurant opens in downtown Bristol

by Kristen Quon

Wed, April 22, 2026 at 6:42 PM
6VIEW ALL PHOTOS
WCYB photo

    topics:

  • Soul food
  • Legit's Kitchen
  • Bristol
  • Christian Rippy
  • Catering
  • To-go meals
  • Southern cuisine
  • Restaurant opening

Downtown Bristol has a new option for soul food and to-go meals with the opening of Legit's Kitchen and Catering.

The restaurant’s menu includes creamy Alfredo, fresh salads and soul food plates with classic sides such as cornbread, collard greens, yams, and mac and cheese.

Owner Christian Rippy said he has always loved to cook and is excited to share that passion with the community. “I offer food for your soul just because I cook it with soul, I cook it with love," he said. "I feel like when you eat my food, I feel like you are just happy. It's fulfilling.”

Legit's Kitchen and Catering is focused on convenient, to-go style meals and is also available for catering.

The restaurant is located at 174 Moore St. and is open Tuesday through Saturday from 1 to 8.
